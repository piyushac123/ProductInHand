Basic procedure for placing an order:-Open website with url www.product-inhand.in. Login as registered user or Register as a new user.;Add content to Search City field and select anyone option of Shop type.;Select preferred shop and add item to list and click PROCEED.;This will display your item-list with required details. Wait for Shopkeepers response.;Now either ACCEPT Response or just DECLINE it.;Visit Shop by given time period and provide Shopkeeper with allocated OTP.;Rate your Experience.
